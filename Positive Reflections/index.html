  • Corruption


    Corruption is not an easy topic. This entity is everywhere in my country, you can see it at school, at your working place, walking on the street. In fact, corruption is omnipresent and omnipotent. To deal with it, one must be able to identify it and realize that a personal attitude change is the only way out of the viscious circle that it represents. But, how can we recognize that corruption is present in our actions? how can we eventually change the social principles with our attitude change? Well, in the next paragraphs I will present some examples that are common in modern life and some suggestions on how to tackle this situations will be also attached. CHIP
